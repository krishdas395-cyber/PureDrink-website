Squeezing the Truth.

How a search for real health turned into a nationwide movement.

Our Story

Hi, I'm Krish Das. Growing up, fresh fruit juice was a staple in my home, but as life got busier, I found myself relying on packaged drinks from local supermarkets. One day, I actually read the label. What I thought was a healthy choice was packed with artificial flavors, heavy sugar syrups, and preservatives.

I realized that millions of people were experiencing the exact same thing. The beverage industry wasn't selling real juice—they were selling flavored sugar water masquerading as health food. I decided to change that narrative.

I started Pure Juice right in my own kitchen. I bought a commercial cold-press juicer and started sourcing the best, nutrient-dense fruits and vegetables I could find from local farmers. No added sugar. No heat treatments burning away the nutrients. Just 100% raw, liquid vitality. What started as a personal quest quickly evolved into a larger movement to bring authentic nutrition back to the people.

Our Mission

To make India drink healthy juice. We are here to completely transform the beverage landscape, stripping away artificial processing and reconnecting people with raw, uncompromised nutrition exactly as nature intended it. No shortcuts, just pure health in every bottle.

About Us

We are a dedicated team of health enthusiasts and flavor purists. Here is the foundation of everything we do:

Cold-Pressed Only

Standard juicing creates heat, destroying vital enzymes. Our hydraulic cold-press method extracts maximum liquid while keeping every vitamin fully intact.

Sourced Locally

We partner directly with sustainable Indian farms. We know exactly where our produce comes from, ensuring it is grown beautifully and responsibly.

Radical Transparency

What is on the front of our label is exactly what is inside the bottle. No hidden ingredients, no "natural flavorings," and absolutely zero added sugars.
